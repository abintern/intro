Angie and Bill International Ltd

Founded in September 1996, Angie and Bill International Ltd is a Taiwan-based trading company specializing in the export of seasonal decorations and outdoor products.

Our main product lines include Christmas light strings, decorative lighting, Christmas trees, inflatables, and various seasonal decorative items, as well as outdoor furniture - sourced from China, Vietnam, Cambodia, and the Philippines to North America and Latin America.
